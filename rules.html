Uploading Rules

  • One Podcast per published work
  • Maximum Podcast length is 20 minutes
  • Podcasters must be an author on the published work
  • Published works must have a registered DOI#
  • Audio file must be mp3 or mp4 format to upload

Instructions:

  1. Uploading is free
  2. Record a research podcast with an audio recording app
  3. Save the completed podcast as an mp3 or mp4 audio file to your device, computer, or cloud drive
  4. Register or login, only registered users can upload a podcast
  5. On PodScholar.org, click "Upload Podcast"
  6. Complete the form with author and manuscript
  7. Upload the mp3 or mp4 file from your computer or cloud drive
  8. Click "Submit Podcast"
